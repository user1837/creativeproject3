Welsh Word Mutator

Croeso! The Welsh Word Mutator is a tool for finding the mutated forms of Welsh words. Mutations in Welsh are systematic sound changes to the first letters of words that occur in specific syntactic and lexical environments. There are three types of mutations: soft, nasal, and aspirate (for a full description of Welsh mutation rules, visit this link). To find a mutation, type the word into the Word box and click on the name of the mutation.

Example words to try: ci, poptyping, tad, brawd, deintydd, gwdihw, mam, llyfrgell, rhan, smwddio, eisteddfod, ffrind, chwaer
